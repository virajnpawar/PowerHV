The Best Products we provide

Performance-Driven Products Built for the Power Industry


We offer a comprehensive range of high-voltage testing and diagnostic products designed to ensure accuracy, safety, and efficiency. From impulse generators to SF6 handling systems, each product supports precision engineering, enabling seamless operation and dependable results across diverse power applications.

HVPD Test System

Detects partial discharge in high-voltage equipment, ensuring insulation integrity, safety, and long-term system performance during operation and diagnostics.

Impulse Voltage Generators

Simulate lightning surge conditions to test dielectric strength of electrical components under high-voltage stress environments accurately and effectively.

High-Voltage Reactors and Dividers

Control and stabilize current or voltage during high-voltage tests, enabling accurate measurement and secure power flow monitoring.

Impulse Current Generators

Generate high-magnitude current pulses used for testing surge resistance of electrical systems against extreme transient events.

Partial Discharge Equipment

Helps identify early-stage insulation faults, reducing potential failures and ensuring improved long-term equipment reliability and safety.

SF6 Handling Systems

Designed for safe SF6 gas filling, recovery, storage, and purification, ensuring gas purity and operational efficiency during maintenance.

Shielding Rooms & PD Laboratories

Provide interference-free environments for precise partial discharge and high-voltage testing under controlled lab conditions.

Shielding Rings & Corona Rings

Equalize electrical fields on high-voltage terminals to prevent corona discharge and surface deterioration in equipment.

HVAC and HVDC Power Source

Delivers stable AC or DC power outputs required for energizing various high-voltage testing and commissioning setups.

HVPD, IVG, and ICG Automation

Automates complex testing processes to improve safety, repeatability, and consistency across high-voltage system evaluations and diagnostics.
