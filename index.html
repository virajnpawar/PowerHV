About Power High Voltage

Leading the Future of High Voltage Services with Precision and Power

Power High Voltage is a service-driven engineering company based in Nashik, India, specializing in high-voltage testing, maintenance, and automation services for the power industry. With a focus on long-term value, safety, and precision, we partner with utilities, OEMs, and industrial clients to deliver tailored solutions that keep critical power systems performing at their best.

Services

The Best Services we provide

Delivering Precision Services for High-Voltage Systems


At Power High Voltage, we specialize in delivering end-to-end service solutions for high-voltage electrical systems across industries. From installation and commissioning to testing, maintenance, and retrofitting, we ensure your power infrastructure runs reliably, efficiently, and safely.
Our team of seasoned engineers and certified technicians is equipped to handle complex service operations for GIS/AIS systems, transformer test setups, impulse generators, and automation consoles. With every project, we bring a service-first mindset, blending global engineering practices with local execution expertise.

GIS/AIS System Repairs & Maintenance Services

Power High Voltage provides complete GIS/AIS repair and maintenance services for utilities, industries, and OEMs ensuring operational reliability across all voltage classes with precision diagnostics and expert technical support.

OEM-Level GIS Service Specialists

Our technicians bring OEM-level expertise, ensuring accurate diagnostics, efficient repairs, and complete restoration of high-voltage GIS systems with precision and care.

Full-Spectrum Voltage Class Coverage

We offer GIS/AIS service coverage from 33kV to 765kV, delivering both preventive and corrective maintenance tailored to different voltage classes.

Professionally Equipped Service Teams

Our teams use calibrated tools like SF6 kits, detectors, vacuum pumps, and certified PPE to ensure safety and service efficiency on every site.

End-to-End GIS/AIS Installation Support

We provide skilled manpower for GIS/AIS erection, testing, shutdowns, dismantling, commissioning, and retrofits executed professionally with proper planning and safety.

Complete GIS Commissioning Solutions

We manage full GIS setup from delivery to charging, including SF6 filling, equipment testing, activation, and on-site commissioning procedures.
