The Best Services we provide

Delivering Precision Services for High-Voltage Systems


At Power High Voltage, we specialize in delivering end-to-end service solutions for high-voltage electrical systems across industries. From installation and commissioning to testing, maintenance, and retrofitting, we ensure your power infrastructure runs reliably, efficiently, and safely.
Our team of seasoned engineers and certified technicians is equipped to handle complex service operations for GIS/AIS systems, transformer test setups, impulse generators, and automation consoles. With every project, we bring a service-first mindset, blending global engineering practices with local execution expertise.

GIS/AIS System Repairs & Maintenance Services

Power High Voltage provides complete GIS/AIS repair and maintenance services for utilities, industries, and OEMs ensuring operational reliability across all voltage classes with precision diagnostics and expert technical support.

OEM-Level GIS Service Specialists

Our technicians bring OEM-level expertise, ensuring accurate diagnostics, efficient repairs, and complete restoration of high-voltage GIS systems with precision and care.

Full-Spectrum Voltage Class Coverage

We offer GIS/AIS service coverage from 33kV to 765kV, delivering both preventive and corrective maintenance tailored to different voltage classes.

Professionally Equipped Service Teams

Our teams use calibrated tools like SF6 kits, detectors, vacuum pumps, and certified PPE to ensure safety and service efficiency on every site.

End-to-End GIS/AIS Installation Support

We provide skilled manpower for GIS/AIS erection, testing, shutdowns, dismantling, commissioning, and retrofits executed professionally with proper planning and safety.

Complete GIS Commissioning Solutions

We manage full GIS setup from delivery to charging, including SF6 filling, equipment testing, activation, and on-site commissioning procedures.

Supply of GIS/AIS Consumables

We supply a wide range of OEM-approved consumables essential for efficient GIS/AIS erection, servicing, and operation ensuring compatibility, performance, and compliance with industry quality standards.

Molecular Sieves (Silica Gel)

Silica gel moisture absorbers keep SF6 gas systems dry, enhancing insulation performance and ensuring long-term operational efficiency.

High-Grade Lint-Free Wipes

Precision-engineered wipes are lint-free and non-abrasive—ideal for cleaning GIS components without static or fiber residue.

Cleaning Agents (IPA)

Fast-evaporating Isopropyl Alcohol effectively removes grease and residue, ensuring clean metallic and insulating surfaces during service work.

Ultra Pure SF6 Gas

We supply certified high-purity SF6 gas with industry-compliant moisture and purity levels for insulation and arc quenching.

O-Rings & Gaskets

Durable sealing solutions made from EPDM, NBR, and Viton ensure leak-proof SF6 gas retention and long-term integrity.

Electrical Contact Grease

Our silicone-based grease improves conductivity, reduces oxidation, and extends the lifespan of terminals and electrical joints.

Desiccant Bags & Breathers

Protect GIS components from moisture and condensation during transit or storage with high-efficiency desiccants and breathers.

SF6 Leak Detection Spray

Foaming leak spray enables accurate visual detection of even the smallest SF6 gas leaks during service work.

Shielding Tapes & Wraps

EMI/RFI shielding tapes help maintain signal integrity and reduce electromagnetic interference in joints and cable terminations.

Copper & Aluminum Lugs

We offer corrosion-resistant, high-quality lugs and termination kits for secure, safe GIS/AIS electrical connections.

Torque Seal & Marking Compounds

Used on fasteners, these compounds verify torque application, enabling visual inspection and audit traceability during maintenance.

PPE & Grounding Accessories

We supply insulated gloves, grounding rods, and shorting cables to ensure complete technician safety during GIS/AIS servicing.

Power System AMC, Repair & Maintenance

Our specialized AMC services include scheduled maintenance, emergency repairs, and system upgrades ensuring safety, uptime, and performance across high-voltage power infrastructure for long-term reliability.

Impulse Power Frequency Source

We offer reliable repairs for high-voltage impulse systems to maintain safety, waveform accuracy, and optimal operation.

Voltage & Current Generator Upgrades

Upgrade control units and enhance safety features to improve performance, stability, and reliability of existing generators.

Impulse Analyzer Calibration

Our expert calibration and maintenance services ensure your impulse analyzers deliver accurate, consistent waveform output every time.

PLC Console Panel Servicing

Retrofit, troubleshoot, and upgrade PLC-based console panels for responsive, smart, and safe high-voltage system control.

AC Resonance System Maintenance

Maintain and repair AC resonance and heat cycle systems to ensure reliable, high-efficiency high-voltage operation.
